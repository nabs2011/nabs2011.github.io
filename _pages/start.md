## Overview

What you'll find on my blog is a collection of articles on topics that I find interesting. I wish I could confidently tell you here that this blog will be about analysis on a consistent topic but in the spirit of transparency there isn't one topic that excites me. What I love is looking at the world as an interwoven web of systems and trends. I love thinking about these systems and how they shape our behaviour. A system can be as complex as the impact of zero-sum thinking on our lives or as simple as what are the skills an analyst needs. 

Although a consistent trend you will see is that I'm pretty data-driven so these posts will be full of analysis. As is the plight of most analysis projects, some of the data sources will be clean and easy to dissect, others may use some proxies or more simplistic analysis. Please reach out if you know of a better data source I can tap into or if you have an idea for a more creative proxy!
